Features

Built for meaningful assessment

More than a quiz tool — a window into how well your students actually understand the material.

Confidence betting

Students wager 1–50 points per answer. High bets signal confidence, low bets reveal uncertainty — automatically.

Real-time statistics

See answer distribution and hesitation rates per question as your class responds. No waiting for results.

Session history

Every session is saved. Review which questions were most missed and average confidence across your class.

No student accounts

Students join with just a pseudo and a code. No sign-up, no friction. They're in the room in seconds.

Full quiz management

Create, edit, and organise quizzes with multiple-choice questions from your teacher dashboard.

Live leaderboard

End every session with a ranked scoreboard. Students see results instantly — keeps energy high.
